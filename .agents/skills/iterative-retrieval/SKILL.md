# Iterative Retrieval (Mirror)

Source of truth: `.cursor/skills/iterative-retrieval/SKILL.md`

## How to apply

1. Start with minimal context and gather only what is needed to unblock execution.
2. Expand context in focused steps instead of bulk-loading directories.
3. Record what was loaded and why to keep reasoning auditable.
