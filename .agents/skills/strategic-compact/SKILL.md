# Strategic Compact (Mirror)

Source of truth: `.cursor/skills/strategic-compact/SKILL.md`

## How to apply

1. Compact context at phase boundaries (audit, implementation, verification).
2. Keep compaction notes actionable: state, decisions, open risks, next step.
3. Avoid over-compaction that removes critical implementation details.
